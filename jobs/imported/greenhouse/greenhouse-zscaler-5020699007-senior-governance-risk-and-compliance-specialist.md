<p data-path-to-node="1">We are looking for a Senior Governance, Risk &amp; Compliance Specialist to join our Technology Risk &amp; Compliance team. You will support the implementation, maintenance, and enhancement of integrated GRC frameworks for FedRAMP and DoD authorizations. Your work will directly influence business strategy by ensuring compliance activities are integrated into broader business processes while supporting our mission as a global cloud security leader.</p>
